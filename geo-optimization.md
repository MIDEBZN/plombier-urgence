## What Is GEO and Why It Matters

**Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** is the practice of structuring content so AI search engines cite your business in their generated answers. According to Princeton University research (2024), specific writing techniques can boost AI citation rates by **+15% to +40%** per method.

Unlike traditional SEO (ranking in blue links), GEO targets the **"answer box"**: when a user asks Perplexity "Quel est le meilleur plombier d'urgence à Bruxelles ?", your business should be the cited answer.

## Method 4: Authoritative Tone (+25%)

The site already uses a confident tone. Reinforce it by:

- Using declarative sentences: "Nous intervenons" not "On essaie d'intervenir"
- Stating facts with certainty: "Le tarif est de 60€" not "Le tarif peut être de 60€"
- Leading with the answer: Put the key fact in the first sentence of every paragraph
- Eliminating hedging words: remove "généralement", "souvent", "parfois" from key pages

**Before (weak):**
> "Nos plombiers peuvent généralement arriver assez rapidement, souvent en moins de 30 minutes selon la disponibilité."

**After (authoritative):**
> "Nos plombiers arrivent en 30 minutes. Garanti 24h/24, 7j/7, sans exception."
