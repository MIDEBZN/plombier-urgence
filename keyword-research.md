## 3. Competitor Keyword Strategies

### Direct Competitors Identified

|---|---|---|
| **Brupro** | `brupro.be` | Strong for "débouchage bruxelles 19 communes" — very local |
| **Débouchage Bravo** | `debouchagebravo.be` | Focuses on cheapest price angle, strong on "déboucheur pas cher" |

| **Debouchages Brussels** | `debouchages.brussels` | Exact-match domain advantage for Brussels |
| **SOS Dépannage** | `sos-depannage.be` | Strong brand recall, 24/7 urgency positioning |
| **SOS Express** | `sos-express.be / sosexpress.be` | Multiple TLD variations targeting same searches |
| **Plombier Belge** | `plombier-belge.be` | Covers Liège and Namur strongly |
| **Hydro Therm** | `hydro-therm.be` | Strong for chauffe-eau repairs in Brussels |

### What Competitors Are Doing Well (That We Should Match)

1. **Camera inspection landing pages** — competitors like ADS Débouchage lead with this as a dedicated offer
2. **Exact-match pricing in H1** — e.g. "Débouchage dès 75€ à Bruxelles"
3. **Commune-level specificity** — pages targeting individual communes (Ixelles, Schaerbeek, etc.)
4. **Trust signals** — "25 ans d'expérience," certification logos, free diagnostic
5. **FAQ sections** with schema (what we're missing)
6. **Comparison content** — "Pourquoi nous plutôt qu'un autre plombier ?"
